Pilau recipe

Pilau

Description

Pilau is a Kenyan coastal dish. It is favourite dish among the swahili speaking people.

The main ingredient is rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cooking Oil
  • Leafy Greens
  • Onion
  • Turmeric
  • Ginger
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Chop the veggies
  2. Wash the rice
  3. Heat the pan
  4. Add cooking oil to boiling
  5. Add onions, stir till golden blown
  6. Add turmeric, ginger
  7. Stir to mix
  8. Add rice and continue to stir
  9. Add water twice the amount of rice
  10. Let it cook for 10minutes
  11. Add chopped green
  12. Stir for 2minutes
  13. Serve
