Ugali Recipe

Description

Ugali is a favourite of the Luyha people of western kenya. It made form maize flour.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of boiling water
  • 3 cups of maize flour

Steps

  1. Heat the water to boil
  2. Add 1 cup of flour
  3. stir
  4. Keep adding small amounts of flour and stirring
  5. Reduce heat and let it cook for about 5minutes
  6. Slice to slices
  7. Serve with meat soup or greenss
