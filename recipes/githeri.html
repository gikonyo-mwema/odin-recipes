Githeri Recipe

Description

Githeri is the most popular dish among the Kikuyu of Central Kenya. Its main ingredients are maize and beans.

Ingredients

  • Boiled maize and beans (githeri)
  • 5 spoonfuls of cooking oil
  • small slices of beef
  • 1l of water
  • spices

Steps

  1. Heat cooking oil on pan to boil
  2. Add chopped onions, garlic, turmeric and other spices
  3. stir to mix
  4. Add slices of beef
  5. Cook for 5minutes
  6. Add boiled maize and beans
  7. Cook 2minutes
  8. Add water for soup
  9. Let it cook for about 5minutes
  10. Food Ready!
